Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 17, 2026

NextSkill Academy ("we", "us", "our") operates nextskillacademy.com. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect when you visit our website, contact us or enroll in a course, how we use it, and the choices you have.

Information We Collect

  • Information you give us: when you call us, request a callback, email us or enroll, we collect the details you provide — typically your name, phone number, email address, career background and learning goals. We use this to advise you honestly and deliver the courses you purchase.
  • Course activity: for enrolled students, we process progress data (modules completed, projects submitted, mentor session notes) to deliver mentorship and track outcomes.
  • Information collected automatically: with your consent, we use Google Analytics 4 to understand how visitors use the site (pages viewed, approximate location, device type). This data is aggregated and does not directly identify you.
  • Call data: if you reach us through a phone number shown in an online advertisement, the advertising platform (such as Google Ads) may record that a call took place, its duration and the caller's number for measurement purposes.

How We Use Your Information

  • To respond to enquiries, run career consults, and deliver enrolled courses and mentorship.
  • To improve our courses, website and services using aggregated analytics.
  • To measure the effectiveness of our advertising, including call-based advertising.
  • To comply with legal obligations and prevent fraud.

We do not sell your personal information. We share it only with service providers who help us operate (payments, video hosting, analytics, advertising measurement), and only to the extent needed.

Cookies and Consent

We use cookies as described in our Cookie Policy. Analytics and advertising cookies are not set until you choose "Accept all" in our cookie banner. Choosing "Necessary only" keeps them off and the site works fully.

Your Rights

Depending on where you live (including under GDPR and CCPA/CPRA), you may have the right to access, correct, delete or export the personal information we hold about you, and to opt out of certain processing. Contact us using the details below — we respond to all verified requests within 30 days.

Data Retention & Security

We keep student records for as long as needed to provide services, honor guarantees and meet legal obligations, then delete them. We use reasonable technical and organizational safeguards, though no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

Children's Privacy

Our courses and website are intended for adults 18+, and we do not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

If we change this policy, we will post the updated version here with a new "Last updated" date.
