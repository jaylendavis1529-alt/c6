About Us

Built by People Who Switched Careers Too

NextSkill Academy exists because online learning promised career change and mostly delivered video subscriptions. We fixed the missing piece: humans.

Our Story

The 4% Problem

Around 2020, our founders — two career-switchers and a former bootcamp instructor — kept running into the same statistic: the completion rate of self-paced online courses hovers in the single digits. Millions of people buy courses; almost nobody finishes them. The content wasn't the problem. The loneliness was.

So in 2021 we built NextSkill Academy around the things that actually get people through a career change: a real mentor who checks in weekly, projects with deadlines that matter, a cohort making the same leap, and career support that starts on day one instead of after graduation. Completion rate today: 81%. That number is the whole company.

We're intentionally not a university. No lectures for lecture's sake, no theory you'll never use. If a module doesn't move you closer to being hire-able, it gets cut.

Our Principles

What We Refuse to Compromise

Humans Over Autoplay

Every student gets a named mentor with real industry experience. Software scales; accountability doesn't. We hire for the second one.

Outcomes Over Certificates

We publish our job-outcome rate (87% within six months) and how we measure it. If we wouldn't hire our own graduates, the course gets rebuilt.

Honesty in Admissions

Advisors are paid the same whether you enroll or not. If a course isn't right for you, we say so — about 1 in 5 consult calls ends with us recommending something free.
